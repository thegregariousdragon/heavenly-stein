Pass with notes.

If off-station, it should identify location, local authority, jurisdiction, travel, communications, and candidate canon.

## Systems check

Needs review if the final draft adds:

- transport consequences;
- communications delay;
- public feed effects;
- fines, liens, or debt;
- force, weapons, disabling, or emergency authority;
- labor or governance consequences.

## Accessibility and sensory check

Pass with notes.
