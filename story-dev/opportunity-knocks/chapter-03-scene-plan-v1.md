## Chapter 3: Predators Adjust Course

## Chapter purpose

Chapter 3 expands the Mirador thread established at the end of Chapter 1. Callum Dresk and Ilya Voren already have the tracker and know *Last Good Haul* is moving. This chapter shows how the Himmelskrug Run announcement changes their timetable, how Northstar pressures from a distance, and how Mirador chooses a public-passenger approach because Der Himmelskrug is bad terrain for crude seizure.

## Scene 1: The Prize Changes the Clock

Location: Mirador transit office, Kestrel Minor waystation, commercial relay booth, or rented logistics room.

Callum and Ilya track *Last Good Haul* after departure. They receive the Himmelskrug Run announcement through route feeds. The race purse changes the risk assessment: if Sven wins, Northstar loses leverage; if Sven becomes publicly visible, station procedure may protect him before Mirador can isolate him.

Function: connect Chapter 2's public announcement to antagonist urgency.

Ending turn: Callum marks the Andersen matter as time-sensitive.

## Scene 2: Northstar's Voice, Not Its Body

Location: same staging space, encrypted creditor call / compliance channel.

A Northstar handler sends updated language about "preservation," "collateral integrity," and "voluntary resolution." The handler never says "threaten Sven" or "seize the ship illegally." They want the result without owning the method.

Function: establish Northstar as remote pressure, not a physical station-side presence.

Ending turn: Mirador understands the real instruction: prevent *Opportunity Knocks* from becoming protected by station process.

## Scene 3: Bad Terrain for Predators

Location: staging space with Der Himmelskrug public access map and Mirador risk notes.

Callum and Ilya review Der Himmelskrug's constraints. Starship Garage is controlled clean-side. Restricted Works is not accessible. Flight Control owns approach/release. Arrival Control logs people. Security responds fast. Compliance cares about liens, identity, and coercion. Public areas are full of cameras, staff, and witnesses.

Function: show Der Himmelskrug as civic infrastructure with teeth.

Ending turn: a direct seizure is rejected as too visible.

## Scene 4: The Passenger Route

Location: booking terminal / commercial transport gate.

Callum and Ilya choose false passenger identities, buy passage, and plan to arrive clean through public channels. Their goal is not to storm the garage. It is to find Sven before he has lodging, legal support, Security awareness, or garage allies, then force a signature or surrender in a semi-controlled edge space near crew or pilot services.

Function: set up their later arrival and Services-adjacent confrontation.

Ending turn: the tracker keeps moving toward Der Himmelskrug. So do they.
