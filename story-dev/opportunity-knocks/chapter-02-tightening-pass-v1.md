# Opportunity Knocks — Chapter 2 Tightening Pass v1

Status: draft-only revision patch.

This file preserves the focused Chapter 2 tightening pass without replacing `chapter-02-draft-v1.md`. Apply these changes when producing `chapter-02-draft-v2.md`.

This file does not create active canon or active chronology.

## Scope

This pass addresses three issues noted after the Chapter 2 draft was saved:

- compress the repeated public-announcement replay in the `Last Good Haul` section;
- correct the Twins' Teeth handling language from yaw/roll to pitch/yaw/roll;
- adjust Ingrid's Lars/Nils phrasing so she speaks from her own relationship frame while still orienting Sven.

## Tightening change 1: compress the announcement replay aboard Last Good Haul

### Problem

The current `Last Good Haul` section repeats too much of the public announcement from the station-side scene. It should keep only the fragments Sven, Freya, Nora, and Ingrid need in order to react.

### Replacement guidance

Keep the arrival of the delayed route bulletin, Nora's first reaction to Der Himmelskrug, Sven's recognition that this is a real sanctioned race, Freya's stillness at the prize purse, and Freya's math.

Cut or summarize most of:

- Heinz's full public welcome;
- Amara's full rule list;
- the full course-sequence replay;
- the repeated sponsor/funding explanation;
- the repeated broadcast commentary.

## Tightening change 2: correct Twins' Teeth attitude-control language

### Problem

The saved Chapter 2 draft still contains one announcement-scene sentence that says:

> The Twins' Teeth require controlled yaw and roll through angled offset gates.

That omits pitch.

### Replacement

Use:

> The Twins' Teeth require coordinated pitch, yaw, and roll through angled offset gates.

Also update any nearby commentary that says viewers should only "watch the roll" when the race is eventually drafted. The better framing is attitude change / coordinated axes / narrow-side presentation.

## Tightening change 3: revise Ingrid's Lars/Nils relationship framing

### Problem

The saved Chapter 2 draft has Ingrid say:

> He said, 'Hide this before Nils frames it in the galley.'

That line orients Sven correctly, but it sounds less natural from Ingrid's point of view because Nils is Lars's father and Ingrid's father-in-law.

### Replacement option

Use:

> "Your father pressed it into my hand after his last race," Ingrid said. "He told me, 'Hide this before my father turns it into galley furniture.'"

Then Sven can still orient the relationship afterward:

> Despite everything, Sven smiled.
>
> "Did Granddad try?"
>
> "Three times."
>
> "Of course he did."

This keeps Ingrid grounded in her own memory while preserving Sven's family perspective.
