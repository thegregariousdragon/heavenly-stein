# Opportunity Knocks — Chapter 11 Scene Plan v1

Status: draft-only planning.

This file does not create active canon or active chronology.

## Chapter 11: Repair Days

## Chapter purpose

Chapter 11 gives the story breath between threat and race.

Several days pass. The chapter should show that *Opportunity Knocks* is restored through labor, not destiny. Sven works with Oskar and the Starship Garage team under supervision. Freya handles legal updates, family paperwork, Crew Support lodging, and communication boundaries. Ingrid helps Nora settle and keeps the Andersen racing memory alive privately, not as media content.

The ending turn is that *Opportunity Knocks* passes inspection — not race clearance, not public registration, and not victory. Inspection means the craft is safe enough to move to the next formal gate.

## Authority lanes to preserve

- **Garage Ops / Oskar:** repair floor, diagnostics, owner-supervised participation, tool discipline, inspection readiness.
- **Engineering / Rolf:** structural/load-path review, station-safe test envelope, repair fixture boundaries.
- **Flight Control / Amara:** movement, practice, racecourse, telemetry, and eventual clearance remain separate.
- **Crew Support / Keisha:** family lodging, privacy, witness support, family-safe language.
- **Compliance / Mara:** legal notices, creditor-contact boundary, evidence package, privacy of financial/medical details.
- **Security / Janelle:** quiet route protection and no direct contact from creditors/contractors.
- **Heinz:** may provide aerospace handling context if useful, but Chapter 11 should not center him or make him the repair authority.

## Scene 1: Days Begin with Rules

Location: Crew Support lodging / Garage Ops intake.

Open with a time shift: three days, or several station mornings. The Andersens are in temporary Crew Support lodging. The family has a routine now: Nora’s breakfast opinions, Ingrid’s practical watchfulness, Freya’s paperwork block, Sven’s supervised garage shift.

The station’s kindness remains procedural:

- route protection;
- no public guest registry;
- scheduled family updates;
- legal-contact boundary;
- Garage Ops access windows;
- Crew Support check-ins.

Function: show safety becoming routine without making it easy.

Ending turn: Sven reports to Garage Ops for another repair day.

## Scene 2: The Repair Is Ugly Work

Location: Starship Garage, Berth C-Gamma-12.

Oskar’s team works through the actual repair tasks:

- full port-forward seam map;
- diagnostic-only microbrace fixture;
- comparison of Nils-era rough/early, Lars cleaner-but-still-honest, and current smooth/late feedback profiles;
- feedback smoothing retune;
- actuator path verification;
- passive test envelope expansion.

Sven assists with archive interpretation and supervised diagnostic tasks. He is useful, not in charge. Oskar gives him bounded tasks and corrects him when memory tries to become certainty.

Function: show labor and prevent magical repair.

Ending turn: the first retune improves the late return but makes the ship’s hand-feel too harsh.

## Scene 3: Honest Is Not Comfortable

Location: cockpit simulation / owner observation console.

The team confronts the central repair tradeoff: the ship must not hide the dangerous delay, but it also cannot punish the pilot with every old vibration.

Heinz may appear briefly or be referenced in a flight-handling note. The key idea: *Opportunity Knocks* must answer honestly, not prettily.

Sven struggles because the harsher feedback feels like family history coming through the controls. Oskar pulls him back to evidence.

Function: connect emotion to technical truth.

Ending turn: Sven finds or interprets one more note from his father that helps separate useful vibration from noise.

## Scene 4: Freya Does the Other Repair

Location: Crew Support lodging / Compliance call room.

Freya handles the legal and practical aftermath:

- protected lodging consent;
- statement follow-up;
- creditor-contact notice;
- evidence package boundaries;
- no direct contact from Northstar/Mirador;
- debt paperwork triage;
- temporary privacy rules;
- family calendar / meals / Nora’s routine.

Mara or a Compliance liaison explains that this does not erase debt, but it does stop hallway coercion and creates a formal channel.

Freya’s emotional beat: she does not get relief; she gets a place to put the fear.

Function: show that paperwork is also repair.

Ending turn: Freya writes a family-safe update sentence for Nora and Sven.

## Scene 5: Ingrid Keeps the Private Legend

Location: Crew Support lodging, quiet evening.

Ingrid tells Nora a small, private story about Nils, Lars, and *Opportunity Knocks* — not a media-ready legend, not a race statistic, but a family memory.

Possible focus:

- Nils was loud and often wrong about furniture.
- Lars listened better than he bragged.
- The ship was never magic; people cared for her badly, well, stubbornly, and again.
- Winning was not the family’s only story.
- Listening was.

Nora asks if the ship is sick. Ingrid says the ship is being listened to by people with tools.

Function: protect the family memory from becoming content.

Ending turn: Sven hears part of it from the doorway and does not interrupt.

## Scene 6: The Clean Answer

Location: Starship Garage.

After several tries, the team gets a better retune: the late return is gone under passive combined attitude profiles, the pilot-feedback path reports sooner without becoming unusably harsh, and the actuator-side path remains clean.

Rolf signs the structural/test-envelope piece. Oskar signs the Garage Ops inspection piece. Amara does not clear race status yet; Flight Control only receives the next gate package.

Function: separate inspection from race clearance.

Ending turn: *Opportunity Knocks* passes inspection.

## Scene 7: Not Cleared, But Ready for the Next Door

Location: Garage Ops / Crew Support bridge.

Oskar tells Sven the result plainly:

- inspection passed;
- no race clearance yet;
- next gate is Flight Control / race eligibility / telemetry review;
- the ship is safer because Sven declared the issue and helped work it.

Sven wants to thank everyone. Oskar permits gratitude only if it does not interfere with cleaning up.

Freya, Ingrid, and Nora receive the family-safe version. Nora asks if the ship is better. Sven says yes, but better means more forms.

Function: end with earned hope, not triumph.

Ending turn: Chapter 12 can move to race clearance and public status questions.
