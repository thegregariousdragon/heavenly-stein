# Opportunity Knocks — Chapter 13 Scene Plan v1

Status: draft-only planning.

This file does not create active canon or active chronology.

## Chapter 13: Kaltmond

## Chapter purpose

Chapter 13 begins the Himmelskrug Run and gives Sven his first major proof on the course.

The public knows *Opportunity Knocks*. The commentators know the legend. The other pilots know the ship’s silhouette. None of that proves Sven. The chapter should show that Sven is not reckless, timid, or merely protected by history. He begins by listening.

The opening chicanes let Sven test the repaired feedback response under real race pressure. He takes legal lines, avoids unnecessary duels, and lets the ship answer before asking more. Some observers mistake this for caution or fear. Maude understands it as listening.

Then Sven commits to Kaltmond’s high-speed gravity-assisted slingshot. The maneuver requires entering Flight Control’s legal gravity corridor at the right angle: too shallow loses the boost, too wide risks disqualification, too deep risks impact. *Opportunity Knocks* answers cleanly and Kaltmond throws him toward the exit chicane with more speed than the field expects.

The ending turn is Sven carrying that unexpected speed into the Kaltmond exit chicane.

## Authority lanes to preserve

- **Flight Control / Amara:** owns course release, telemetry, flags, abort authority, corridor legality, and disqualification decisions.
- **Race Ops / Nessa Rook:** manages race feed status, entrant order, timing, public display integration, and race logistics.
- **Broadcast / Ren Calder and Maude Kess:** interpret the race publicly, but do not know private repair, debt, medical, security, or family details.
- **Family viewing / Freya, Ingrid, Nora:** protected, delayed/filtered, child-safe where needed; no public camera access.
- **Garage Ops / Oskar:** may watch telemetry, but does not clear race decisions once the ship is on course.
- **Heinz:** may watch and understand the line technically, but does not direct Flight Control or claim Sven as public proof.

## Scene 1: Green Means Work

Location: Sven cockpit / race start corridor / Flight Control.

Start exactly after Chapter 12’s green light.

The field launches. Other ships show personality immediately: aggressive acceleration, clean professional starts, public-feed favorites asserting position. Sven does not try to win the first second. He keeps *Opportunity Knocks* legal, steady, and honest.

Function: establish that race start is disciplined, not cinematic release.

Ending turn: Sven feels the repaired feedback answer under real acceleration for the first time.

## Scene 2: Public Feed Wants a Story

Location: broadcast booth / public levels / protected family room.

Ren frames the start. Public feed notices *Opportunity Knocks* is not immediately doing something legendary. Some crowd noise shifts toward confusion.

Maude warns against cheap interpretation:

- Sven is not slow.
- He is listening.
- Old ships tell the truth in the first corrections, not in the launch scream.
- Opening chicanes punish people who confuse public expectation with race position.

Family-safe room: Nora asks if Papa is behind. Freya says he is where Flight Control says he is allowed to be. Ingrid says listening sometimes looks like waiting.

Function: set public impatience against private understanding.

Ending turn: Sven enters the opening chicanes.

## Scene 3: Opening Chicanes

Location: early Knotward Run course.

Sven takes the opening chicanes cleanly:

- legal line;
- no unnecessary duel;
- measured acceleration;
- micro-corrections through pitch/yaw/roll as needed;
- telemetry green;
- repaired feedback return honest.

A more aggressive pilot tries to force a bad choice or tempt Sven into a public-looking pass. Sven refuses the bait and exits with less drama but better setup.

Function: show control before spectacle.

Ending turn: Maude says he is buying the right to be fast later.

## Scene 4: Flight Control Measures, Not Cheers

Location: Flight Control.

Amara watches Sven’s telemetry. She does not praise him publicly. She sees:

- clean corridor compliance;
- no telemetry masking;
- no remote-abort concern;
- cautious but deliberate line choice;
- repaired feedback behaving under race load.

Nessa Rook handles Race Ops timing and public display. Sven’s sector may not look spectacular yet, but his exit vectors are good.

Function: show official systems recognizing competence before public does.

Ending turn: Kaltmond corridor opens in his forward plot.

## Scene 5: Kaltmond in the Window

Location: cockpit.

Kaltmond appears not as romance but as a gravity problem with a legal corridor wrapped around a barren moon.

Sven reviews the tradeoff:

- too shallow: no boost;
- too wide: corridor violation / disqualification risk;
- too deep: impact risk;
- too proud: bad exit setup;
- too cautious: loses race.

He remembers the repair problem without turning it into ghosts: late feedback used to make this dangerous because exit timing would arrive during the next correction. Now the ship answers honestly.

Function: turn technical repair into race decision.

Ending turn: Sven commits to the corridor entry angle.

## Scene 6: The Slingshot

Location: Kaltmond corridor / cockpit / broadcast.

Sven enters the legal gravity corridor with a line that looks too close to casual viewers and too precise to Maude.

Maude explains the stakes:

> Too shallow, he loses the boost. Too wide, Flight Control throws the flag. Too deep, Kaltmond keeps him.

Ren keeps public viewers oriented. The public feed shows speed climbing. Flight Control tracks legality.

Sven does not fight the moon. He lets Kaltmond take what it is allowed to take, then asks *Opportunity Knocks* for the exit correction.

Function: old legend flickers awake in a new pilot’s hands.

Ending turn: the repaired feedback returns on time.

## Scene 7: More Speed Than Expected

Location: Kaltmond exit / Flight Control / family room.

Kaltmond throws *Opportunity Knocks* out hotter than the field expects. Race Ops timing updates. Ren realizes the conservative start was setup, not fear. Maude is not surprised, but she is impressed.

Family room:

- Nora sees the line jump and asks if that is good.
- Freya says she thinks so.
- Ingrid says, “Now he has to keep it.”

Flight Control does not celebrate. Amara watches the exit chicane risk rise with the speed.

Function: convert listening into visible advantage.

Ending turn: Sven carries more speed than expected into the Kaltmond exit chicane.
