Status: draft-only planning.

## Chapter 14: The Teeth and The Gauntlet

## Chapter purpose

Chapter 14 is the middle climax of the race.

Chapter 13 proved that Sven was calculating, not reckless, and that *Opportunity Knocks* could answer cleanly under Kaltmond load. Chapter 14 proves the repair can hold under sustained, compounding pressure.

The chapter begins with Sven carrying more speed than expected into the Kaltmond exit chicane. He must convert speed into a usable line without bleeding away the gain or overdriving the repaired ship. Then he aims for the Twins’ Teeth: an angled, offset asteroid gap requiring coordinated pitch, yaw, and roll so *Opportunity Knocks* presents her narrowest safe profile while still carrying speed.

The Teeth are not the finish of the challenge. The Teeth-to-Gauntlet chicane immediately asks another question. The Gauntlet follows: a sanctioned high-precision corridor of rapid gates, tight turns, sensor-shadow pockets, and repeated corrections. The repaired frame resonance must not return.

The ending turn is Sven exiting The Gauntlet with the repair holding.

## Authority lanes to preserve

- **Flight Control / Amara:** owns course legality, flags, telemetry, abort authority, and traffic/corridor safety.
- **Race Ops / Nessa Rook:** tracks timing, sector deltas, entrant positions, public display, and telemetry integration.
- **Broadcast / Ren Calder and Maude Kess:** can describe risk and skill but do not know private repair details.
- **Family viewing / Freya, Ingrid, Nora:** protected view only; child-safe overlay may simplify risk without lying.
- **Garage Ops / Oskar:** may watch the technical feed, but does not clear or direct racecourse decisions.
- **Heinz:** may understand the line and be emotionally/technically invested, but does not direct Flight Control or turn the moment into his public proof.

## Scene 1: The Exit Chicane Asks First

Location: Kaltmond exit chicane / Sven cockpit / Flight Control.

Sven exits Kaltmond hotter than expected. The exit chicane punishes pilots who leave Kaltmond too fast, too wide, or too proud.

Sven resists the urge to keep every bit of speed. He bleeds exactly enough to stay legal and preserve setup for the Twins’ Teeth.

Flight Control tracks the chicane speed advisory. Amara does not praise. She watches for pride.

Function: show speed only matters if Sven can keep using it.

Ending turn: Sven exits the chicane still carrying meaningful advantage.

## Scene 2: Public Feed Catches Up

Location: broadcast booth / public levels / family room.

Ren frames the Kaltmond gain. Maude explains that the gain is not “free speed” because it must be converted into the next legal line.

Family room:

- Nora asks if Papa is winning.
- Freya answers carefully: he gained time.
- Ingrid adds that gaining time is only useful if he does not spill it.

Function: keep public excitement from flattening the risk.

Ending turn: the Twins’ Teeth appear on the race plot.

## Scene 3: The Teeth Are Not a Slot

Location: approach to Twins’ Teeth.

The public graphic initially makes the Teeth look like a gap. Maude corrects this:

- the two asteroids are angled and offset;
- the legal line is not straight;
- the ship must present its narrowest safe profile;
- pitch, yaw, and roll must coordinate precisely.

Sven reads the shape as a presentation problem, not a dare.

Function: make the Teeth technically specific and avoid “just fly between rocks.”

Ending turn: Sven commits to the Teeth line.

## Scene 4: Showing the Teeth Her Narrow Side

Location: Twins’ Teeth passage / cockpit / Flight Control.

Sven threads the Teeth.

He coordinates pitch, yaw, and roll under load. The repaired feedback response answers fast enough that he is not flying an echo. The ship reports vibration, but not late return.

Flight Control sees legal passage. The public feed may go briefly quiet.

Possible Maude line:

> He is showing the Teeth her narrow side.

Function: turn the repaired handling issue into visible race skill.

Ending turn: Sven clears the Teeth, but the exit angle is already asking for the chicane.

## Scene 5: The Next Line Is Already Asking

Location: Teeth-to-Gauntlet chicane.

Survival is not enough. A bad Teeth exit compromises the Gauntlet.

Sven must recover attitude and line quickly, managing speed without panicking or overcorrecting. The ship answers cleanly but firmly. He is tired now. The course begins compounding.

Function: transition from one spectacular hazard to sustained precision.

Ending turn: Sven enters The Gauntlet with enough line to fight for it, not enough comfort to relax.

## Scene 6: The Gauntlet Hates Drama

Location: The Gauntlet / Flight Control / broadcast.

The Gauntlet is dense, legal, famous, and hated:

- rapid gates;
- tight turns;
- sensor-shadow pockets;
- course pylons;
- repeated pitch/yaw/roll/thrust corrections;
- limited recovery time.

Ren explains the sequence for viewers. Maude focuses on fatigue and accumulated correction debt.

Sven must keep asking small, correct questions. *Opportunity Knocks* keeps answering on time.

Function: sustained pressure, not one big stunt.

Ending turn: telemetry shows the repaired frame resonance is not returning.

## Scene 7: The Repair Holds

Location: Garage Ops technical viewing / Flight Control / cockpit.

Oskar and the garage team watch the technical feed. They do not cheer early. They watch the repaired feedback profile under repeated load.

The old late-return signature does not appear.

Heinz may watch from Feli-controlled space and understand the significance, but the scene should stay with Sven, the ship, and the crew who repaired her.

Function: pay off repair labor without making it magic.

Ending turn: Oskar allows one small sign of relief, not celebration.

## Scene 8: Out of the Gauntlet

Location: Gauntlet exit / racecourse / family room.

Sven exits The Gauntlet with the repair holding. He has not necessarily won yet, but he has survived the sustained precision test and kept usable speed.

Family room:

- Nora asks if the hard part is over.
- Freya cannot answer.
- Ingrid says, “One hard part is over.”

Flight Control confirms legal exit.

Function: land the middle climax and set up final acceleration / The Last Pour.

Ending turn: *Opportunity Knocks* exits The Gauntlet with clean telemetry and acceleration recovery ahead.
