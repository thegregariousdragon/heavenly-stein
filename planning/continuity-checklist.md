# Continuity Checklist

Use this checklist before drafting or revising new stories in the continuity rebuild.

## Source order

1. Read `CANON_INDEX.md`, `README.md`, and `MANIFEST.md` for package status.
2. Check `quick-reference/QUICK_START.md`, `quick-reference/CANON_LOCKS.md`, and `quick-reference/RECENT_CHANGES.md`.
3. For Der Himmelskrug scenes, use `quick-reference/STATION_BLOCKING_GUIDE.md` and `canon/08-der-himmelskrug-station.md`.
4. For character behavior, use `quick-reference/CHARACTER_QUICK_CARDS.md`, `canon/02-heinz-schmitt.md`, `canon/03-family.md`, `canon/04-core-relationships.md`, `canon/09-station-leadership.md`, and `canon/17-character-personality-and-scene-fingerprints.md`.
5. For ships, transport, weapons, clean-side/restricted-side routing, law, economy, corporations, locations, media feeds, and labor, use the relevant numbered canon files.
6. Use `canon/19-tested-story-precedents.md` for system principles only, not old event history.

## Continuity rebuild rule

Previous story plots, chronology, and scene blocking are not active canon. Character specifics and worldbuilding canon remain active.

## Must-check topics

- Heinz/Feli relationship status and voice.
- Flight Control authority.
- Arrival Control and clean-side/restricted-side routing.
- Transport and personnel movement.
- Weapons, force, nonlethal effects, and public safety.
- The Green Room / Grünraum and cannabis rules.
- The Spare Tap and staff privacy.
- Residential privacy and school/Learning Center access.
- Station geography and bubble elevator limits.
- Labor, governance, law, media, and economic consequences.
