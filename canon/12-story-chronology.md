## Active chronology

### 1. `Opening the Stein`

`Opening the Stein` is the first locked event in the rebuilt active chronology.

It covers Der Himmelskrug’s official public opening: the first ordinary public arrivals, opening remarks, first public pour, mixed-but-fair media framing, and station staff proving the public-opening systems under pressure.

Key locked event facts:

- The first public wave is released from Arrival Control into Hospitality Level 1 after normal clearance/declaration handling.
- No public visitor appears directly in the taproom, Residential, school, brewery production, Command, or other restricted spaces.
- Flight Control keeps opening traffic in safety order; media, historic desire, founder status, and private-traffic impatience do not modify traffic law.
- Hospitality Level 2 catches a food-label mismatch before guest exposure and corrects it before service, reinforcing that guest-facing allergen information is operational safety infrastructure.
- Priya contains and resolves a private Residential rest/assignment dispute during opening prep, reinforcing that Residential privacy and livability are not subordinate to public spectacle.
- A live performer segment is quietly adjusted to protect performer dignity and privacy; rehearsal audio remains in the program while the public focus shifts to first arrival, station reveal, and first pour. This is not public scandal canon.
- Because the ceremonial line is not clearing cleanly, the first public pour comes from a verified backup line at the same bar. The moment becomes an early public example of process outranking spectacle.

Candidate-only details from the story remain candidate-only until later promotion. This includes Mirelle Voss as a recurring character, Janna as recurring Comms/event-support staff, Marn-Taal as a durable colony/culture/location reference, the specific food dish, Naya, and individual first-wave visitor reactions.

Sanaa El-Masri and Lian Zhou are already active canon in `canon/09-station-leadership.md`; their opening-story actions use their established roles and do not require new character promotion.
