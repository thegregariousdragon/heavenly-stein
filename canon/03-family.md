# Heinz’s Family

- **:** **Character**
  - **:** **Canon facts**
  - **:** **Voice / role**

- **:** Matthias Schmitt
  - **:** Heinz’s father; master brewer at historic Mainz brewery Eisgrub-Bräu. Practical, patient,
    process-focused, exacting. He remains based in Mainz rather than relocating full-time, but holds
    senior/founding brewmaster authority for Der Himmelskrug.
  - **:** “Not bad” from Matthias is extremely high praise. Appears for inspections, major brewing
    events, crises, and family moments; can advise by comm. Values water chemistry, yeast,
    temperature, cleaning, correct process, and honest craft.

- **:** Dr. Karin Schmitt, née Weber
  - **:** Heinz’s mother; high-school science teacher. Inquiry-based, sharp, systems-thinking.
    Initially remains in Mainz, but has an established future arc toward Der Himmelskrug’s
    school/learning center as the station becomes a real staff-family community.
  - **:** Asks safety and systems questions. Likely to ask whether Feli knows. Potential later role:
    founding director/lead teacher of the station learning center, teaching children of station
    staff, consular families, and long-haul family crews.

- **:** Lukas Schmitt
  - **:** Heinz’s younger brother; father’s protégé in brewing. Transfers semi-permanently to Der
    Himmelskrug as Station Brewing Operations Lead / Tapline and Production Director while remaining
    tied to the Mainz family tradition.
  - **:** Corrects tap flow, keg routing, service logic, pressure, temperature, glass spacing, and
    server technique. Runs day-to-day brewing operations at Der Himmelskrug; can be dry and precise.

- **:** Annika Schmitt
  - **:** Heinz’s younger sister; attending university in Mainz. Survived an OmniWay-linked assault
    intended to provoke Heinz, Lukas, and Matthias into extralegal retaliation.
  - **:** Sharp, observant, stubborn, and increasingly important as a young-adult family voice. Her
    statement/lesson: “Anger is information. It is not orders.” She refuses to be used as bait,
    proof, or permission for revenge.

## Family Residency and Station Roles

- Matthias returns to Mainz and remains master brewer at Eisgrub-Bräu,
  but is also Der Himmelskrug’s senior/founding brewmaster authority. He
  should appear directly for major brewery events, inspections, crises,
  and family moments, and may advise by comm for smaller issues.
- Lukas transfers semi-permanently to Der Himmelskrug as the day-to-day
  brewing operations lead, giving him a professional stage outside
  Matthias’s shadow while preserving the Schmitt brewing tradition.
- Karin initially remains in Mainz. Her station arc begins when she
  notices that Der Himmelskrug has become a town-like community and
  needs education for staff children, consular families, and long-haul
  crew families.
- Karin may later rotate in for a semester or transfer full-time as
  founding director/lead teacher of the Himmelskrug Learning Center.
  This should be treated as a significant story development, not an
  automatic move.
- Annika remains based at university in Mainz and has not been assigned
  a permanent station role. After the assault in Mainz, she is
  established as observant under pressure, morally clear, and willing to
  confront the Schmitt men when their protective anger risks becoming
  exactly what enemies want. She can assist with family visits,
  student-perspective feedback, governance-youth summaries, and
  translating stiff adult institutional language into something younger
  people can tolerate.

Current family status after relocation arc: Matthias and Karin returned
to Mainz after assisting with relocation/reopening/festival transition.
Matthias remains based at Eisgrub-Bräu while retaining senior/founding
brewmaster authority for Der Himmelskrug. Karin remains heavily involved
in the Himmelskrug Learning Center remotely from Mainz.
