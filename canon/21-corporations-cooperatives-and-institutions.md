# Corporations, Cooperatives, Guilds, and Institutions

## Canon status key

## Corporate ecology principles

Corporations and institutions in this setting should grow from place, route, labor, law, and infrastructure. A company should usually have a home, a route dependency, a labor culture, a regulatory pressure, and a reason Der Himmelskrug cares about it.

Avoid making every company a villain. The universe is more useful if organizations fall into several categories:

- Predatory corporations that hide harm behind contracts.
- Useful corporations with moral blind spots.
- Honest firms that are expensive, slow, or politically awkward.
- Cooperatives and guilds that protect workers but can become rigid or territorial.
- Public trusts and care institutions that do good work while depending on imperfect funding.
- Media institutions that shape truth, rumor, pressure, and grief.
- Shell firms and contractors that allow larger actors to deny responsibility.

The strongest corporate conflicts should involve maintenance, labor, routing, debt, insurance, food safety, media framing, emergency authority, and public trust.

## Hard canon organizations

### OmniWay Leisure Infrastructure

**Status:** hard canon.  
**Type:** discount-route infrastructure corporation.  
**Base:** likely Lagrange Nine / Earth corporate channels, with route platforms and shell offices. Exact headquarters can remain flexible unless needed.  
**Known assets:** Meridian Rest Platform, crew-bunk facilities, discount-route hospitality infrastructure, media-placement channels, shell contractors, influence operations.  
**Canon role:** major corporate antagonist and cautionary contrast.

OmniWay Leisure Infrastructure represents the bad version of frontier hospitality: efficient branding, thin staffing, margin-driven maintenance, outsourced responsibility, aggressive legal framing, and cheerful public surfaces over unsafe systems.

OmniWay should not be written as cartoonishly incompetent. Its danger is that it understands contracts, perception, route dependency, discount pricing, subcontracting, and plausible deniability. It can make bad infrastructure look normal until people die.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** rival model, antagonist, cautionary contrast, and source of accountability pressure.

**Story uses:** corporate liability, hidden maintenance failures, influence operations, safety theater, discount-route exploitation, shell contractors, ex-employee witnesses, legal accountability, restitution fights.

**Continuity lock:** OmniWay uses contracts, perception, route dependency, subcontracting, and plausible deniability. It should remain competent enough to be dangerous.

### Mirador Asset Protection Group

**Status:** hard canon.  
**Type:** private contractor / asset-protection force.  
**Base:** OmniWay-linked contractor network; exact headquarters flexible.  
**Canon role:** thug-like contractor force used in false asset-protection actions.

Mirador Asset Protection Group is the sharp end of corporate denial. It operates in the language of asset recovery, contractual enforcement, facility security, and creditor protection, but its practical function is intimidation, seizure, and control.

Mirador proves that corporate pressure in this universe is not limited to lawyers and public relations. It can become armed, coercive, and physically dangerous.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** threat actor and investigation target through contractor-force and asset-protection abuse.

**Story uses:** private force, contract abuse, false liens, hostage-like infrastructure seizures, "security" used as corporate violence, IFP intervention thresholds.

**Continuity lock:** Mirador is linked to OmniWay's ecosystem but should remain legally deniable enough to create investigation and accountability problems.

### Frontier Truthline

**Status:** hard canon.  
**Type:** former far-right / populist outrage media feed; covert influence operation.  
**Base:** shell offices and rented production spaces; likely Lagrange Nine legal shells plus distributed production.  
**Canon role:** exposed OmniWay-directed influence operation.

Frontier Truthline is a bad-faith outrage media operation that weaponizes real anxieties about private authority, frontier infrastructure, family influence, and cultural legitimacy. Its main feed is no longer a major active outlet, but remnants, splinter personalities, archives, lawsuits, migrated viewers, successor feeds, or monetized grievance channels may persist.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** discredited hostile influence feed whose damage remains part of public-memory and legal aftermath.

**Story uses:** media laundering, bad-faith framing, conspiracy migration, reputation damage, defamation suits, leaked data, post-collapse remnants.

**Continuity lock:** Frontier Truthline is no longer a major active feed, but its damage and audience migration remain story fuel.

## Media organizations as institutions

Media editorial posture remains governed by `canon/11-media-feeds.md`. These entries track location, structure, commercial pressure, and institutional story use.

### INN / Interstellar News Network

**Status:** hard canon.  
**Type:** major interstellar corporate news network.  
**Base:** New York, Earth.  
**Editorial authority file:** `canon/11-media-feeds.md`.

INN is a large, legacy interstellar news network with institutional weight. It values order, defense readiness, private property, strong borders, disciplined institutions, and route stability.

As an institution, INN has access to old Earth finance, law, diplomacy, route-policy experts, military analysts, and corporate advertisers. Its scrutiny of Der Himmelskrug can be uncomfortable but should not automatically be bad faith.

**Story uses:** hard interviews, accountability segments, security framing, private-authority skepticism, serious but conservative institutional pressure.

### Galaxy News

**Status:** hard canon.  
**Type:** major interstellar corporate news network.  
**Base:** Los Angeles, Earth.  
**Editorial authority file:** `canon/11-media-feeds.md`.

Galaxy News is a large interstellar news network based in Los Angeles, close to Earth's media, entertainment, labor, culture, and public-image industries. It values humanitarian systems, cultural pluralism, worker safety, mutual aid, and accountability.

As an institution, Galaxy can be glossy without being shallow. Its coverage can humanize workers, families, residents, and cultural communities while still asking serious governance questions.

**Story uses:** labor-focused coverage, human-interest segments, public-image pressure, accountability interviews, festival-cultural reporting with political undertones.

### Civic Meridian

**Status:** hard canon media feed; institutional base soft canon.  
**Type:** centrist process-focused media institution.  
**Base:** Lagrange Nine Commercial Ring.  
**Editorial authority file:** `canon/11-media-feeds.md`.

Civic Meridian is careful, timeline-oriented, and neutral to a fault. Its Lagrange Nine base gives it access to arbitration records, legal analysts, insurers, route-policy panels, corporate hearings, and executive briefings.

Its institutional weakness is proximity to people who turn harm into process language. It can be accurate and useful while missing moral scale.

**Story uses:** clean summaries, hearing coverage, both-sides framing, procedural blindness, useful timelines.

### Redline Commons

**Status:** hard canon media feed; institutional base soft canon.  
**Type:** decentralized activist-media collective.  
**Base:** distributed network; major node at Kestrel Minor.  
**Editorial authority file:** `canon/11-media-feeds.md`.

Redline Commons is not a corporate tower. It is a distributed anti-corporate, pro-worker, anti-private-authority media collective with contributors across worker housing, small-hauler ports, student networks, union halls, and route-user forums.

A major Kestrel Minor node fits its concern with debt, liens, private infrastructure, and small-operator pressure.

**Story uses:** partially right critiques, ideological overreach, worker testimony, debt investigations, pressure campaigns, slogans that sting because they are not always wrong.

### RouteCast / BeaconWatch

**Status:** hard canon media/safety feed; institutional base soft canon.  
**Type:** route-conditions and urgent-alert institution.  
**Base:** Relay Haven plus distributed beacon bureaus.  
**Editorial authority file:** `canon/11-media-feeds.md`.

RouteCast is a pilot-facing route-conditions feed. BeaconWatch is its urgent-alert format. RouteCast should feel close to working crews, beacon technicians, service stations, weather analysts, and Flight Control professionals.

Relay Haven gives RouteCast moral texture: route safety as survival work, not content.

**Story uses:** solar storms, beacon failures, route closures, emergency holds, safe-harbor advisories, technical truth correcting political noise.

### Starlight Tonight

**Status:** hard canon media feed; institutional base soft canon.  
**Type:** entertainment and celebrity interstellar feed.  
**Base:** Los Angeles, Earth.  
**Editorial authority file:** `canon/11-media-feeds.md`.

Starlight Tonight covers music, festivals, fashion, celebrity, romance, awards-style moments, and public image. It can trivialize serious context, but it also captures cultural moments political feeds miss.

Its Los Angeles base connects it to entertainment industries, talent agencies, festival promotion, and public-image machinery.

**Story uses:** viral clips, Heinz/Feli public attention, music guests, festival episodes, celebrity pressure, accidental trivialization.

### Across the Table

Across the Table covers chefs, brewers, agricultural sourcing, foodways, service culture, allergen safety, staff meals, and the systems behind feeding people across settled space.

Glasswater gives it a base in life-support, water, aquaculture, and food-system seriousness rather than pure lifestyle coverage.

### Open Route

**Status:** hard canon media feed; institutional base soft canon.  
**Type:** travel and destination feed.

Open Route covers ports, planets, stations, accessibility, practical itineraries, family travel, route culture, visitor behavior, and destination infrastructure.

A Port Irie headquarters gives it warmth and cultural travel legitimacy. A Lagrange Nine sales office gives it access to advertisers, travel boards, and polished commercial partnerships.

## Soft canon economic institutions

### Vesta Crown Fabrication Guild

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** unionized fabrication guild / industrial supplier network.  
**Base:** Vesta Crown.  
**Sector:** precision fabrication, pressure hull components, certified replacement parts, tool assemblies.

The Vesta Crown Fabrication Guild produces expensive, slow, excellent industrial components. It represents the honorable version of industrial stubbornness: documented tolerances, shop-floor authority, inspection culture, and refusal to rush pressure-rated work.

The guild is not romanticized as perfect. It can be territorial, slow, blunt, and politically difficult. But if a Vesta Crown stamp is real, engineers trust it.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** supplier, auditor, or inconveniently correct outside expert.

**Story uses:** forged components, supplier disputes, union politics, safety delays, Rolf respecting someone who argues properly.

### Pelican Yardmasters Cooperative

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** shipyard cooperative / repair authority.  
**Base:** Pelican Yards.  
**Sector:** freighter refit, salvage conversion, emergency repair, used components.

Pelican Yardmasters Cooperative is a practical ship-repair organization owned or heavily governed by working yardmasters, tug operators, certified mechanics, and dock engineers.

It exists to keep ships flying without pretending every captain can afford ideal repairs. Its moral tension is that compromise is sometimes necessary, but unsafe compromise kills people.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** repair partner, independent-hauler support node, or outside assessor after ship damage.

**Story uses:** repair disputes, salvage refits, independent-hauler aid, insurance fights, ugly but legal patch jobs, pilots arguing with mechanics.

### Lagrange Nine Arbitration Exchange

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** arbitration, insurance, and commercial dispute institution.  
**Base:** Lagrange Nine Commercial Ring.  
**Sector:** route contracts, cargo liability, insurance disputes, settlement frameworks.

The Lagrange Nine Arbitration Exchange is useful and bloodless. It resolves disputes that would otherwise paralyze trade, but it also speaks the language of exposure, liability, and settlement rather than grief, repair, or justice.

It should not be evil. It should be institutionally comfortable with outcomes that leave everyone unsatisfied and nobody fully accountable.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** external arbitration venue and legal-pressure site.

**Story uses:** insurance delays, cargo-priority arbitration, cargo-priority arbitration, Feli and Clara/Klara in legal-language combat.

### Glasswater Life Systems

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** life-support and water systems company.  
**Base:** Glasswater.  
**Sector:** filtration, aquaculture systems, algae media, bioplastics, station water management.

Glasswater Life Systems is a technically excellent supplier in the life-support and bio-materials sector. Its products are reliable, but its culture can be condescending toward colonies and stations it considers biologically sloppy.

The company is a good source of non-villain conflict: a supplier can be right about contamination and wrong about people.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** supplier, inspector, or expert witness for life-support and biological contamination issues.

**Story uses:** food safety, life-support audits, mislabeled botanical cargo, station water scares, a polite expert making everyone angry by being correct.

### Caldera Thermal Materials Combine

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** industrial materials combine.  
**Base:** Caldera City.  
**Sector:** heat shielding, ceramics, geothermal equipment, refinery components, industrial catalysts.

Caldera Thermal Materials Combine produces essential dirty-side materials: heat-shield tiles, thermal ceramics, high-temperature components, abrasive mineral products, and industrial catalyst containers.

Its cargo is valuable and often inconvenient. The Combine's pressure comes from clients who need the shipment now, not after Customs finishes being "dramatic" about dust.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** supplier, dirty-side cargo user, and occasional customs-pressure source.

**Story uses:** dirty-side routing conflict, contamination pressure, hazardous cargo shortcuts, labor heat-safety disputes, supplier leverage.

### Kestrel Mutual Credit

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** small-hauler credit union / mutual lender.  
**Base:** Kestrel Minor.  
**Sector:** ship repair loans, bridge credit, lien restructuring, small-operator finance.

Kestrel Mutual Credit is not saintly, but it is less predatory than the commercial debt brokers around it. It exists because small haulers need credit or they stop flying.

Its dilemma is that kindness does not make damaged ships cheaper. It can help some captains and still foreclose on others.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** small-operator finance partner, critic, claimant, or pressure source after disablements and repair disputes.

**Story uses:** debt pressure without easy villains, repair financing, Redline Commons investigations, predatory competitors.

### Saint Brigid's Lantern Care Trust

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** medical/hospice care trust.  
**Base:** Saint Brigid's Lantern.  
**Sector:** medical transfer, hospice, grief care, chaplaincy, family support, charity transport.

Saint Brigid's Lantern Care Trust runs care infrastructure for long-haul crews, families, medical transfers, and end-of-life cases. It is one of the setting's best institutions, but it still depends on funding, insurance cooperation, charity transport, and political goodwill.

Its stories should be quiet and morally sharp. The antagonist may be a denied claim, a missing signature, a delayed transport, or a corporation trying to look compassionate without paying.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** care partner, grief-support resource, medical transfer destination, or conscience witness after disasters.

**Story uses:** medical transfer disputes, grief support, Dr. Santos scenes, Feli handling language around loss.

### Zion Drift Cultural Exchange Cooperative

**Status:** soft canon.  
**Type:** cultural, music, food, and hospitality cooperative.  
**Base:** Port Irie, Zion Drift.  
**Sector:** performers, spice vendors, rum makers, hospitality staff, festival programming.

The Zion Drift Cultural Exchange Cooperative helps artists, cooks, growers, distillers, and performers negotiate fair participation in off-world festivals and station programming.

It protects culture from being turned into cheap atmosphere. It can also become protective, suspicious, or politically demanding when outsiders get careless.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** festival partner, supplier, performer advocate, and cultural-respect pressure point.

**Story uses:** festival contracts, performer pay, cultural respect, Tariq/Davi/Amara connections, hospitality ethics.

### Tannenfall Agricultural Standards Board

**Status:** soft canon, though conceptually tied to hard canon Tannenfall.  
**Type:** public-private standards body.  
**Base:** Larkspur Landing, Tannenfall.  
**Sector:** agricultural labeling, biosecurity, orchard exports, weather-control compliance, food-chain certification.

The Tannenfall Agricultural Standards Board is the kind of organization everyone mocks until it saves an entire harvest. It certifies cargo, labels, plant material, soil, water, packaging, and weather-control interfaces.

It is not a corporation exactly, but it has enormous economic power.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** compliance partner, customs pressure source, and food-chain safety authority.

**Story uses:** customs conflict, food labeling, cannabis/botanical products, "annoying rules were correct," Elsbeth Ruun pressure.

### New Bavaria Brauerbund

**Status:** soft canon, tied to hard canon New Bavaria.  
**Type:** brewing guild / cultural trade association.  
**Base:** New Bavaria.  
**Sector:** beer, brewing standards, yeast, festival licensing, apprenticeships, cultural authenticity.

The New Bavaria Brauerbund protects brewing standards and Bavarian-derived festival traditions across New Bavarian settlements and export markets.

It can support Der Himmelskrug's legitimacy while also worrying that the station's fame may distort tradition into spectacle.

**Relationship to Der Himmelskrug:** legitimacy partner, supplier, standards voice, and occasional critic.

**Story uses:** brewing disputes, festival standards, Schmitt family tension, yeast supply, cultural legitimacy, comedy about labels that matter too much until they matter exactly enough.
