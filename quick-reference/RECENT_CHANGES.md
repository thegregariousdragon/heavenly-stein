## Station layout rewrite retained

The redesigned Der Himmelskrug layout remains active:

1. Crown
2. Observation
3. Entertainment
4. Hospitality Level 1
5. Hospitality Level 2
6. Retail
7. Residential
8. Services
9. Starship Garage
10. Restricted Works

Key retained station decisions:

- Crown is the fixed four-quadrant civic cap.
- Observation remains open with chandelier and atrium void.
- Entertainment is the venue level.
- Hospitality Level 1 handles arrivals, taproom, upper brewery, cafés/restaurants, and press.
- Hospitality Level 2 handles food hall, major bar, lower brewery, hotel, personal services, and The Spare Tap’s brewery-service adjacency.
- Retail is the final public level and includes The Green Room / Grünraum.
- Residential has the Wohnhof and quadrants.
- Services is controlled staff/pilot/operational support.
- Bubble elevators serve public atrium levels only.

## Station layout completion: Services, Starship Garage, and Restricted Works

- Services is now locked as controlled staff/pilot/crew/Medical/recovery/documentation/Starship Garage transition support, not a public convenience floor.
- Formal garage names are now Starship Garage and Restricted Works. Clean garage / dirty garage remain staff slang only.
- Starship Garage handles normal clean-side ship berthing, clean cargo, inspection, recharge, crew transition, and non-hazardous ship support.
- Restricted Works handles decon, quarantine, hazardous salvage, dirty repair, artifact/anomaly intake, restricted ordnance/rearming, evidence/compliance/security, industrial drones, and heavy engineering.
- Consumable starship-weapon rearming belongs in Restricted Works, not ordinary Starship Garage berths.
- Lass ma einen bauen has a Starship Garage pride-of-place berth plus controlled public-viewable feed for approved special events.
- Nur Für Den Fall may use a controlled Starship Garage long-stay cutter berth when safed, declared, and logged.
